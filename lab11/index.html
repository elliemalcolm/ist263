Ellie's Biography


Personal Information

My name is Ellie Malcolm. I am a junior at Syracuse University majoring in advertising and minoring in psychology, creative writing, and information management & technology. I also have an interest in film and production and am looking to pursue a career within marketing in the film and music industry.

Since coming to Syracuse University, I have worked as the lead on the marketing team for the largest volunteering event and hold the record for most volunteer signups, I have been promoted to executive producer on two social media shows within a student-run television show, and am involved in several other clubs while maintaining honors status and being named on the Deans list.

In my personal life, I enjoy reading all sorts of books, staying active, cooking, and watching movies and tv shows. All of this, I enjoy doing with my friends and family. Currently, I have become more interested in skincare, updating my wardrobe, and finding low-budget decorations for my apartment.

As someone who can struggle with the overwhelmingness of life, my goal is to be able to find a balance in both my personal life and career, with the hope to achieve a combination of success and content.
